About Paradigm Shift

What is Paradigm Shift?

Paradigm Shift is the cutting-edge financial advisory platform that empowers you to find, hire, and collaborate with vetted fiduciary financial advisors, enhanced by advanced AI technology. Supported by the Opportunity Fund and founded by the SoftBank Group, Paradigm Shift's mission is to grow and protect your wealth through unparalleled client experiences and innovative solutions.


With Paradigm Shift, you can seamlessly connect with financial advisors across the United States, tailored to your unique financial needs and goals.

What sets Paradigm Shift’s network of advisors apart?

At Paradigm Shift, advisors undergo rigorous due diligence to join our network.


Every advisor holds a CFP®, CFA, or CPA certification, is an accredited independent advisor, has a minimum of 5 years of experience, and is a subject-matter expert in assessing your finances.


Most advisors prioritize recommending what’s "suitable" for you and your family, not what’s "best." While this distinction may seem minor, over the long run it can significantly impact your ability to build long-term wealth.


Imagine a scenario where an advisor is choosing between two mutual fund options:


  • Option 1: Higher client fees and a $20,000 commission check for the advisor once you invest.
  • Option 2: Low-cost, tax-efficient fund with low client fees but no advisor commissions.

Our vetting process ensures that advisors are legally unable to recommend Option 1 to you. At Paradigm Shift, we believe that's just common sense.


You deserve better. Paradigm Shift delivers. Why not interview a select group of advisors who lead with financial planning, specialize in working with families, and provide transparent fees?

Where are Paradigm Shift Financial advisors located?

The financial advisors in our network are located in Europe. Most Paradigm Shift Advisors also work remotely and digitally, regardless of your location.

How do we connect you with Financial Advisors?

The difference between Paradigm Shift and other financial advisory platforms is that we focus on quality over quantity.


Our matching process begins with a review of your financial goals and ends with you receiving a curated selection of advisors tailored to your unique situation.


Through AI technology, we take the guesswork out of finding you a great match.

What is Paradigm Shift’s Satisfaction Guarantee?

We will refund all advisor fees if you’re unsatisfied with the advisor you’ve found and hired through our platform.


We work hard to curate the wealth planners we allow to join our network. This is why we are confident in the quality of the service you will receive. Terms and conditions apply

How do I get connected with with an advisor from the Paradigm Shift Network?

Knowing where you stand financially is important, and you want an advisor who keeps you in the loop.


  1. Complete our quick Get an Advisor form
  2. Our algorithm provides you with a selection of three fiduciary financial advisors for you to choose from.
  3. Schedule time to interview your matches.

Our Concierge team is here to support you throughout your interview process, answering questions, and helping you connect with the best fit for your needs.

Additional FAQs

What makes Paradigm Shift’s technology unique?

Paradigm Shift integrates advanced AI technology to streamline and enhance the financial advisory process.


Our platform uses sophisticated algorithms to match you with the most suitable advisors based on your financial goals, preferences, and risk tolerance.


This technology not only saves you time but also ensures that you receive personalized, high-quality advice from experts who are best suited to meet your needs.

How does Paradigm Shift ensure the security of my financial information?

At Paradigm Shift, the security of your financial information is our top priority.


We employ state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access and breaches.


Our platform is regularly audited and updated to comply with the highest standards of data protection and privacy, ensuring that your personal and financial information remains safe and confidential.

What are the costs associated with Paradigm Shift’s services?

Paradigm Shift is committed to transparency when it comes to fees.


Our platform itself is free to use, and you will be clearly informed about any fees associated with the financial advisors you choose to work with. Advisors typically charge a percentage of assets under management or a flat fee, depending on the services provided.


There are no hidden fees, and all costs will be clearly outlined before you commit to any advisory services.

Can I switch advisors if I’m not satisfied?

Yes, at Paradigm Shift, we understand the importance of finding the right fit.


If you are not satisfied with your current advisor, you can request to be matched with a different advisor. Our team is here to support you through the process to ensure you find an advisor who meets your expectations and financial needs.


Your satisfaction is our priority, and we are committed to helping you find the best financial partner for your journey.
